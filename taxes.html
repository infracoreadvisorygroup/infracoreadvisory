Tax Services

Tax preparation for individuals, families, and business owners

InfraCore Advisory Group helps with simple W-2 returns, returns with dependents, self-employed taxes, 1099 income, Schedule C business returns, extensions, amendments, and prior-year filings.

Tax services available

Whether your return is simple or more involved, tax pricing is based on forms, dependents, credits, business income, and filing complexity.

Basic W-2 Return

Starting at $75 for simple W-2 returns with one job, no dependents, no credits, and no additional forms.

W-2 with Dependents

Starting at $125–$150 for W-2 returns with dependents, basic credits, or multiple W-2s.

Individual Tax Return

Starting at $175–$225 for most individual returns with items like unemployment, retirement income, dependents, or basic deductions.

1099 / Self-Employed

Starting at $250–$300 for simple 1099 income with organized records and basic expense tracking.

Schedule C / Business Return

Starting at $300–$500+ for self-employed workers and business owners with business income, expenses, multiple 1099s, or higher complexity.

Extensions & Catch-Up Help

Extension filing starts at $50. Prior-year returns, amendments, and catch-up filing are available depending on the situation.

Add-on tax services

Some returns require additional forms, corrections, or extra filing support. Add-on pricing depends on what is needed and how much review is required.

  • Tax extension filing — starting at $50
  • Additional state return — $50
  • Amendments — $100–$200
  • Prior-year return — starting at $150 per year
  • ITIN application — $150
  • IRS notice help — starting at $150

Who tax services are for

Tax preparation is available for more than just business owners. InfraCore helps individuals, families, employees, contractors, and small business owners file with better organization and less stress.

  • Individuals with simple W-2 income
  • Families claiming dependents and credits
  • 1099 contractors and gig workers
  • Self-employed business owners
  • People needing prior-year filing support
  • Anyone needing an extension or amendment

Why clients come to InfraCore

Taxes can feel overwhelming when records are scattered, forms are missing, or business income is mixed with personal activity. InfraCore helps organize the information needed so filing is clearer and easier to understand.

The goal is not just filing a return. It is helping you understand what is being filed, what may be missing, and what to track better going forward.

What this helps prevent

  • Last-minute filing stress
  • Missing W-2s, 1099s, or tax forms
  • Confusion around dependents or credits
  • Poor expense tracking for 1099 income
  • Unfiled prior-year returns
  • Guessing what to set aside for taxes
