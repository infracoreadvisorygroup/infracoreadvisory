Popular Workshop Topics

Workshops are built around the financial questions business owners most often ask when they are trying to stay organized and improve their business systems.

Starting a Business the Right Way

Learn what financial records to track from day one, how to organize your books, and the financial habits that make running a business easier.

Expense Tracking & Write-Offs

Understand common deductions business owners miss and how to properly track expenses throughout the year.

Cleaning Company Financial Tracking

Workshops designed for cleaning companies covering mileage tracking, supplies, referral discounts, and contractor payments.

1099 Contractor Basics

Learn what business owners should understand when working with contractors and how to track contractor payments correctly.

Industry-Specific Workshops

Some workshops are designed for specific industries like salons, bakers, service businesses, and independent contractors.

Tax Readiness for Business Owners

Understand what records to organize before tax season and how bookkeeping supports smoother tax filing.
