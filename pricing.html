Engagement Packages

InfraCore Advisory Group does not offer one-size-fits-all pricing. Each engagement is structured based on the scope of work, business complexity, and level of ongoing support required.

Below is an overview of our standard engagement types. Specific scope and fees are outlined after an initial consultation.

Business Blueprint

Designed for: Individuals preparing to launch a business or restructure an existing concept.

  • Business concept review and feasibility analysis
  • Revenue and pricing strategy
  • Startup or restructuring budget
  • Projected Profit & Loss statements
  • Break-even analysis
  • 30/60/90-day implementation roadmap

One-time strategic engagement.

Business Build-Out

Designed for: Businesses ready to move from planning into active operations.

  • Entity and tax structure guidance
  • Accounting system setup
  • Sales tax setup (if applicable)
  • Operational workflow design
  • Initial financial tracking and reporting setup
  • Funding readiness and compliance preparation

Structured project-based engagement.

Ongoing Business Support

Designed for: Operating businesses requiring consistent financial oversight and compliance support.

  • Monthly bookkeeping and reconciliation
  • Financial reporting
  • Business tax preparation
  • Compliance reminders and advisory support
  • Ongoing operational guidance

Monthly engagement. Scope determined during consultation.
