Events & Workshops

Practical workshops for business owners

InfraCore Advisory Group offers educational events designed to help owners better understand bookkeeping, expense tracking, tax readiness, business organization, and the financial habits that support stronger businesses.

Learn what to track and why it matters

These workshops are built around practical questions business owners actually ask. Instead of vague information, the goal is to leave with clearer understanding and usable next steps.

Workshop topics

Topics are designed around the financial and operational areas that commonly create confusion for business owners.

Starting a business

Helps owners understand what should be tracked early and how to build stronger financial habits from the beginning.

  • Business setup basics
  • What records to keep
  • Financial organization habits

Expense tracking and write-offs

Covers common deductions, tracking habits, and the kinds of expenses owners often miss through the year.

  • Mileage and vehicle tracking
  • Software and subscriptions
  • Promotions and marketing expenses

Tax readiness

Helps owners understand what should be organized before tax deadlines and how stronger records reduce stress later.

  • Year-end organization
  • Common tracking gaps
  • Preparing cleaner records

Industry-based workshops

Sessions can be shaped around specific business types and the financial questions they commonly run into.

  • Service-based businesses
  • Salons and beauty businesses
  • Cleaning companies and contractors
