Client Engagements

Our work is structured through defined engagement types. Many businesses identify with more than one area — the purpose of this page is to help clarify where advisory support may be most valuable.

Foundational Engagement

Designed for new or transitioning businesses that need structure, clarity, and proper positioning before moving forward.

  • I am starting a new business or restructuring an existing one
  • My financial records are incomplete or inconsistent
  • I need guidance on setup, compliance, or tax positioning
  • I want a clear roadmap before making decisions
  • I am looking for focused, short-term advisory support

Ongoing Advisory Relationship

Designed for established businesses seeking ongoing clarity, organization, and strategic support.

  • I have an active business with recurring activity
  • I need consistent bookkeeping and financial organization
  • I value year-round advisory access
  • I want proactive guidance, not just compliance
  • I am seeking a long-term advisory relationship

Tax & Compliance Engagement

Designed for individuals and businesses focused on filings, compliance, and tax-related decision support.

  • I need business and/or individual tax preparation
  • I want guidance on estimated payments
  • My filings involve multiple forms or entities
  • I want to reduce surprises and avoid issues
  • I may transition into advisory support later
It is common for businesses to identify with more than one engagement type. Selections above are not used to place you into a predefined package, but to highlight areas where advisory support may be appropriate.
