Client Engagements

Our engagements are structured to reflect business complexity, advisory involvement, and level of ongoing support. Review the criteria below to determine alignment.

Foundational Engagement

Designed for new or transitioning businesses that need structure and clarity.

  • I am starting a new business or restructuring an existing one
  • My financial records are incomplete or inconsistent
  • I need guidance on setup, compliance, or tax positioning
  • I want a clear roadmap before moving forward
  • I am looking for focused, short-term advisory support
This engagement establishes the foundation required before ongoing advisory or bookkeeping begins.

Ongoing Advisory Relationship

Designed for established businesses seeking ongoing clarity and support.

  • I have an active business with recurring activity
  • I need consistent bookkeeping and financial organization
  • I value year-round advisory access
  • I want proactive guidance, not just compliance
  • I am seeking a long-term advisory relationship
Ongoing engagements are structured monthly and evolve as the business grows.

Tax & Compliance Engagement

Designed for individuals and businesses focused on filings and compliance.

  • I need business and/or individual tax preparation
  • I want guidance on estimated payments
  • My filings involve multiple forms or entities
  • I want to reduce surprises and avoid issues
  • I may transition into advisory support later
Tax engagements may stand alone or support other advisory relationships.

Next Step

If one or more of these engagement types feels aligned, the next step is a brief consultation to confirm scope and structure.
