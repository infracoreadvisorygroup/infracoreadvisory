About InfraCore Advisory Group

InfraCore Advisory Group is a professional services firm providing structured business planning, operational build-out, bookkeeping, and licensed business tax preparation for small businesses.

We work with entrepreneurs and business owners at every stage — from idea to operation — helping build the financial and operational infrastructure required for long-term stability and informed decision-making.

Our role is advisory and supportive. Clients retain full ownership and operational control of their businesses.

Services

Business Blueprint

Strategic planning for new businesses, including feasibility analysis, pricing strategy, startup budgeting, projected Profit & Loss statements, break-even analysis, and 30/60/90-day implementation planning.

Business Build-Out

End-to-end setup support including entity and tax structure guidance, accounting system setup, sales tax setup where applicable, financial tracking systems, starter website structure, basic marketing materials, funding readiness support, and first-month bookkeeping.

Ongoing Business Support

Monthly bookkeeping, account reconciliation, financial reporting, compliance reminders, and preparation of business tax returns. Payroll services available as an add-on.

Our Process

Initial Consultation: Evaluation of business goals and current position.

Planning & Structure: Development of financial models and operational systems.

Implementation: Setup of systems and compliance frameworks.

Ongoing Support: Continued bookkeeping, tax compliance, and advisory services.
